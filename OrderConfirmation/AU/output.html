Frequently-Asked Questions
When will I hear from Castlery next?
You will next hear from us when we are getting your order ready for collection by the carrier. This should be shortly before the beginning of your Delivery ETA window shown on your order confirmation.
When can I expect my order to arrive?
You can expect to receive your delivery within the Estimated Delivery dates shown above. In the event of unforeseen delays, we will contact you as soon as possible. For more information, please click here.
How do I track my order?
Thanks to our interactive live tracking system, checking the status of your order is easy!
Just log in to your account, select ‘Orders’, then the item you wish to review, and we’ll take you to a tracking page that will give updated information as your delivery progresses.
Alternatively, look out for an email containing tracking information to use on the relevant delivery partner’s website.
How do I view or change my order?
To view your order just log in to your account on our website and select ‘Orders’. You cannot alter an order once it has been placed and payment processed. Please contact us through help.au@castlery.com if you need to alter/cancel or place your order on hold. Please note additional warehousing charges may be applicable.
Have any other questions about your order or getting ready for your delivery?
Feel free to refer to the delivery FAQ here , complete with a Checklist with more important information.
