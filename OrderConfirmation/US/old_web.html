Frequently Asked Questions
When will I receive my items?
You can expect to receive your shipment within the Estimated Delivery dates shown above and in your Castlery Account.
If your order is able to be shipped earlier than expected, it will be processed automatically and there is nothing you need to do. If there are any unforeseen delays, we will contact you as soon as possible.
How will my items be delivered?
Homeware items and selected furniture items packed in medium-sized cartons will be delivered as soon as the items are available via our courier partners such as Fedex. You will receive an email containing your Courier Tracking Number and a link to check shipment status. Courier deliveries do not require a signature, and if you are not present to receive your delivery, the items will be left at your ground-floor doorstep. For addresses located above the ground-floor, items will be left at the entrance of the lift or foot of the stairs.

Other furniture items will be scheduled and delivered by a two-person delivery team according to the Delivery Service you’ve selected per shipment (Standard, Room of Choice or White Glove). For full details, please visit our Delivery Page.
What should I prepare ahead of delivery?
For Non-Courier deliveries, our delivery partners will contact you to arrange a delivery day and time window. They may also contact you a few hours ahead on the day of delivery as confirmation.
Please note the following:
  • Please measure and ensure that your items will fit through doors, staircases and lifts in their packaging before you place your order.

  • Ensure you have a photo ID available. Our delivery partners are required to verify your identity for security purposes.

  • Protect your flooring and surroundings ahead of delivery. Our delivery partners will not be responsible for any damages.

  • Check your delivered item(s) carefully before our delivery partners leave the premises. Any damage(s) detected should be immediately reported and indicated on the delivery document. Signing of the delivery document serves as proof of acceptance of the condition of the item(s) at the time of delivery.
For full details, please visit our Delivery Page.
How do I view my order? What if I want to change or cancel my order?
Log in to your Castlery account online to view your most up-to-date Order Status.

If you decide to change or cancel your order, please contact us before the dispatch date and no additional charges will be incurred. To process such requests, email us at help.us@castlery.com. We are available from Monday to Friday, 10am to 6pm, Pacific Standard Time.

Any change or cancellation after warehouse dispatch will incur return shipping and logistics charges. For full details, please visit our Sales and Refund Page.
