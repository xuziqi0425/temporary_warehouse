Frequently Asked Questions
When will I receive my items?
There is a 'Deliver By' estimate for each item. This is an indication of when your items are expected to arrive in our warehouse. We will send out an SMS and Email with a delivery schedule link closer to your chosen date for you to select the actual delivery date and timeslot. A delivery confirmation email will be sent to you after we have confirmed your delivery timing.
How do I view my order? What if I want to change or cancel my order?
Visit online Order Status to view the most up-to-date status. If you decide to change/cancel your order, please notify us at least 5 working days before the agreed delivery date and no additional charges will be incurred. Any cancellation/change of order within 5 working days of agreed delivery date will be subject to restocking fees. For more details, visit our Terms & Conditions.
